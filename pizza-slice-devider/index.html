Pizza Slice Calculator

About

  • Tired of always getting the small slices of a pizza?
  • Ever wanted to share a pizza equally?
  • Take back your right to equally cut slices with the equal pizza slice calculator!
  • Just select the size of your pizza and the number of slices you'd like to have.
  • The equal pizza slice calculator will give you a neat preview of your pizza, its slices and even the correct angle to cut your slices.
